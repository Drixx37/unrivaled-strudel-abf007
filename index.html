Required Documentation

✓ Valid State Photo ID or Driver's License

Plus ONE of the following documents to prove authority:

• Certificate of title
• Valid vehicle insurance certificate
• Tax collector's receipt & registration card
• Notarized power of attorney
• Court order
• Notarized insurance proof of loss/theft claim
• TXDOT registration information matching your ID
• Current lease or rental agreement
• Law enforcement agency credentials
• TDLR approved form (available at facility)

TDLR approved forms are available at our facility and online at www.tdlr.texas.gov

Retrieving Personal Items

✓ What You Can Do:

The vehicle owner or authorized representative may remove personal belongings from the impounded vehicle after presenting:

  • • Proper identification
  • • Required documentation (as listed above)

✗ What Is NOT Allowed:

Vehicles may NOT be dismantled. The following may NOT be removed:

  • • Engine or engine parts
  • • Tires or wheels
  • • Installed stereo systems
  • • Any permanently installed vehicle parts

The facility may require you to sign a receipt documenting all items removed from your vehicle.
